Mid-Autumn Festival - Childhood Memory

Date: Sep. 18th 2016

Where: WPI Campus, 100 Institute Road, Worcester MA 01609


This time we are going to introduce some typical Chinese games, such as Rubber Band Skipping (跳皮筋), Long Rope Skipping (跳长绳) and Shuttlecock (踢毽子). We plan to hold the event outdoor if weather permits.
