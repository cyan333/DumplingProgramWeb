About Dumpling Program

Dumpling Program is an organization under CSA that consists of families who have adopted children from China. Most families knew little about their children's heritage, so we hold the workshop once a term to share Chinese culture with them through interactive activities between Dumpling families and WPI students. Then the children come to know about and appreciate the culture of their birthplace through games, cooking events and all types of interactions with WPI students.
